Is this program legitimate?

Absolutely! We've partnered with leading insurance providers to offer this cash back program. You get the exact same policy at the exact same price, but we share our commission with you as cash back. This is a common practice in many industries, but rarely offered in insurance until now.

How much cash back will I receive?

The amount varies by policy type and provider, but typically ranges from 15-25% of your first year's premium. For example, if your annual premium is $3,000, you could receive $450-$750 back. Higher premiums often qualify for higher percentage rewards.

When will I get my cash back payment?

Payments are typically issued 60-90 days after your policy is approved and in force. This waiting period ensures the policy remains active. You'll receive your payment via check or direct deposit, depending on your preference.
