Our Story

Transforming Insurance in Nepal

Founded in 2024, Merobima began with a simple yet powerful idea: insurance should protect families while also putting money back in their pockets. Our innovative cash back program was born from this vision, revolutionizing how Nepalese families approach insurance.

What started as a small team of insurance professionals passionate about fairness has grown into a trusted partner for thousands of families across Nepal. We've returned over ₹25 lakh in cash back to our customers while providing the same excellent coverage they would get directly from insurers.
